# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety
1.  Never share your Aadhaar number, PAN, or bank details over 
      phone or email unless you initiated the contact
2.  Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable 
      two-factor authentication (2FA)
3.  Regularly check your bank statements for any transactions 
      you did not make
4.  Avoid oversharing personal details (date of birth, phone 
      number, address) on social media
5.  Always log out of accounts when using shared or public 
      computers
6.  Keep your phone and laptop software updated to patch 
      security vulnerabilities

## Financial & UPI Safety
1.  Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone — not even 
      someone claiming to be from your bank
2.  Only use official UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) downloaded 
      from the official Play Store or App Store

## If You Are a Victim
-   Report immediately at the **National Cyber Crime Portal**: 
  cybercrime.gov.in
-   Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** (available 24x7)
