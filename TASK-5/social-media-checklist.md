# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's
1. Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles updated and professional.
2. Use your college email for all academic and professional sign-ups.
3. Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable 
   with your professor seeing it.
4. Follow industry professionals and companies in your field 
   to stay updated.
5. Use social media to showcase your academic projects, 
   certifications, and achievements.

## ❌ DON'Ts
1. Don't share personal information like your address, 
   phone number, or Aadhaar details publicly.
2. Don't post negative comments about your college, 
   professors, or classmates online.
3. Don't share OTPs, passwords, or banking details 
   with anyone — even in private messages.
4. Don't engage with or spread unverified news and 
   forwards on WhatsApp or Twitter/X.
5. Don't use informal language, slang, or emojis when 
   communicating with professors or recruiters online.
